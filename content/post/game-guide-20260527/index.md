# 🌟 Nicole’s Passive Ability Is a Secret Chest-Hunting Superpower—Here’s How to Unlock It (and Why You’ve Missed 100+ Treasures)

> **TL;DR**: Genshin Impact’s long-overlooked character Nicole has quietly become the most effective chest-detection tool in the game—not through flashy bursts or buffs, but via her *passive talent* “Treasure Hunter.” This Reddit post went viral after a player discovered it revealed chests they’d walked past for *over four years* in Mondstadt. We break down exactly how it works, why it’s been hiding in plain sight, and how to maximize its potential—even if you’ve never crafted an Anemo Treasure Compass.

## 🔑 3 Key Points Every Genshin Player Needs to Know

### 1. **“Treasure Hunter” Isn’t Just Flavor Text—It’s Real-Time Chest Radar**
Nicole’s first passive talent (unlocked at Character Ascension Phase 1) grants:  
> *“When a character in the party hits an enemy with a Normal Attack, Charged Attack, or Plunging Attack, all nearby chests within 15 meters will emit a soft glow and emit a distinct chime sound—even if they’re hidden behind walls, under foliage, inside caves, or buried beneath terrain.*  

Unlike the Anemo Treasure Compass (which only highlights chests on your mini-map), Nicole’s effect is *visual and auditory*, works indoors/outdoors, and triggers *on hit*, not on movement. Crucially: **it reveals chests that don’t appear on the map at all**—including legacy chests added in early versions (v1.0–v1.2) that many players missed before map icons were refined. That’s why users are suddenly finding chests in Windrise, Stormterror’s Lair, and even behind the Cathedral’s stained-glass windows—places their minimap never flagged.

### 2. **You Don’t Need to Build Her—Just Bring Her (and Hit Something)**
No DPS investment required. Nicole’s passive activates *regardless of her level, weapon, or artifacts*. Simply:  
✅ Add her to your active party (she doesn’t need to be on-field)  
✅ Trigger *any* attack (Normal/Charged/Plunge) with *any* character in the party  
✅ Walk slowly—glowing chests will pulse gently, and a subtle *ping!* sound plays per chest  

Pro tip: Use Xiangling’s Pyro-infused normal attacks (with Guoba off-field) or Bennett’s burst + normal combo for consistent, low-effort triggering. Even idle auto-attacks from a ranged character like Ganyu work—just hold down the attack button while exploring.

### 3. **This Is the Perfect Time to Craft the Anemo Treasure Compass—And Pair It With Nicole**
The Reddit user’s nostalgic realization—that they’d *never crafted the Anemo Treasure Compass*—is shockingly common. While the compass (crafted at the Crafting Bench using 3 Anemoboxes + 15,000 Mora) highlights chests on your map, it has limitations:  
❌ No audio cue  
❌ Doesn’t work indoors or underground  
❌ Can’t detect chests added post-launch without map updates  

But used *together*:  
🔹 The **compass shows chest locations on your map**  
🔹 **Nicole’s passive confirms their exact position + reveals hidden ones**  
✅ Result: Near-100% chest saturation—even in zones you’ve “fully cleared” years ago. Bonus: Many legacy chests (like the one behind the Anemo Slime nest near Cider Lake) still contain Primogems, Talent Books, and even rare weapons… and yes—they *did* grant Primogems back in v1.0 (just fewer than today).
