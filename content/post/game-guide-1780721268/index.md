Their clothing features subtle motifs: frayed silk cuffs resembling old Liyue Guild apprentices’ uniforms, embroidered wave patterns echoing the *Fishing Association*, and one child’s pendant bearing a stylized *Qingce Mountain* fox motif. Crucially, none wear Vision symbols or display elemental auras — aligning with HoYoverse’s consistent rule that Visions manifest only after profound emotional catalysts. HoYoverse has signaled a thematic pivot toward “civilians as pillars of stability” (per miHoYo’s 2024 Kyoto Game Dev Panel).
