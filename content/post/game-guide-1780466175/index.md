The core philosophy behind the “Which would you rather have?” question isn’t about raw power rankings — it’s about *contextual synergy*. For example:  
- Choosing between **Kazuha (Anemo) and Nahida (Dendro)** isn’t just DPS vs. support; it’s asking: *“Does my current team lack off-field damage amplification (Nahida) or crowd control + elemental resonance flexibility (Kazuha)?”*
