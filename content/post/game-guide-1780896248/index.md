## 🔑 Three Definitive Proofs That Early Archons Were *Extremely* Strong

### 1. **Raiden Shogun (Original) — The Island-Splitting Thunderstrike**  
The Musoujin Gorge isn’t just a scenic rift—it’s forensic proof of divine violence. According to *Archon Quest Chapter II*, Raiden Shogun (not Ei, but her unnamed predecessor) engaged the Serpent Lord Orobashi in a battle so cataclysmic that her ultimate technique—**Musou no Hitotachi**—*bisected Yashiori Island* in a single strike. The resulting chasm remains geologically unstable centuries later, with residual Electro energy still crackling along its cliffs. Crucially, this feat predates Ei’s ascension and her subsequent refinement of the technique—it was raw, unfiltered Archon-level power wielded *in combat*, not ritual.

### 2. **Zhongli (Original) — The Skyfall Burial of a Sea God**  
The Guyun Stone Forest isn’t ancient geology—it’s a tombstone. As confirmed in *Zhongli’s Character Story I* and the *Liyue Harbor Archive*, the original Geo Archon didn’t merely defeat Osial, the Sea God; he **summoned colossal rock pillars from the heavens** to pin and entomb the deity beneath the seabed. These pillars didn’t erode—they *fused* with the ocean floor and rose over millennia into the towering, gravity-defying spires we explore today. This wasn’t localized terraforming; it was celestial engineering: manipulating mass, density, and tectonic forces across an entire coastal region in real time.

### 3. **Venti (Original) — The Mountain-Flipping Gale That Forged the Abyss**  
Musk Reef—the submerged ruins housing the Spiral Abyss entrance—isn’t natural coral growth. Per *Windblume Festival lore documents* and *Diluc’s story entries*, the original Anemo Archon didn’t just “scatter leaves”—he **hurled an entire mountain range sideways** during his war against the Abyss Order. The reef is the *eroded base* of that displaced peak, now submerged and warped by Abyssal energy. Its unnatural curvature, inverted strata, and persistent wind vortexes (visible in-game during storms) are all direct physical signatures of that primordial act—a feat requiring control over atmospheric pressure, kinetic force, and geological mass on a scale dwarfing even Venti’s current “Breeze of Freedom.”
