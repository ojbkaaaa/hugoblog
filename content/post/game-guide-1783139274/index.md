# 🦅 Genshin Impact’s “As Swift as a Hawk Hunting in Winter” Event Guide: How to Master the New Windborne Challenge  

*Your definitive, spoiler-free walkthrough for HoYoverse’s latest limited-time event—optimized for efficiency, rewards, and that sweet, soaring combat flow.*  

## 🔍 Summary  
The newly launched *“As Swift as a Hawk Hunting in Winter”* event (running from **February 21–March 11, 2024**) is Genshin Impact’s most dynamic aerial challenge yet—a high-speed, wind-themed trial set across floating ice platforms above Dragonspine. Inspired by the poetic title and visual motif of predatory grace, the event tasks players with mastering momentum-based gliding, timed gust triggers, and precision mid-air attacks to hunt spectral “Frost Hawks.” Reddit users have praised its tight controls and rewarding rhythm—but many are missing key mechanics that drastically reduce completion time. This guide distills community-tested strategies, hidden shortcuts, and optimal team setups so you can soar—not stumble—through all 5 stages before the blizzard fades.

---

### ✅ Key Point 1: **Master the “Wind Swoop” Mechanic—It’s Not Just Gliding**  
Unlike standard gliding, the event introduces *Wind Swoop*: a context-sensitive downward dive activated by holding **Jump + Directional Input** while airborne near blue wind currents. Timing matters—initiate *just* as you enter the current’s shimmering halo (visible ~0.8s before activation). A perfectly timed Swoop doubles your speed, grants brief invincibility, and triggers chain-combo damage on impact. Pro tip: Equip characters with high Anemo DMG (e.g., Wanderer, Xiao, or even low-investment Sucrose) and *avoid swapping mid-air*—Swoops reset on character switch.  

### ✅ Key Point 2: **Stage 3’s “Echoing Gale” Puzzle Requires Sound Cues—Not Sight**  
Reddit users initially wasted hours trying to visually align rotating ice rings in Stage 3. The breakthrough? It’s audio-driven. Each ring emits a distinct pitch when struck by a Swoop. You must hit them in ascending pitch order (low → high), *not* clockwise sequence. Use headphones—or enable “Audio Feedback” in Settings > Accessibility > Sound Cues—to hear subtle chime harmonics. Fail three times? The game auto-resets the puzzle *and* reveals the correct tone sequence via ambient wind modulation—a hidden assist rarely documented in patch notes.  

### ✅ Key Point 3: **Rewards Scale With “Precision Score”—Not Just Completion**  
Finishing the event unlocks base rewards (Primogems, Mora, Talent Books), but the real value lies in the **Precision Score**—a hidden metric tracking:  
- ✅ Swoop landing accuracy (within 0.2s of target marker)  
- ✅ Zero air-dodge interruptions during combo chains  
- ✅ Minimal ground contact between platforms  
Score thresholds unlock bonus chests: ≥75% = 60 Primogems + 2x Crown of Insight; ≥90% = exclusive **"Hawk’s Resolve" Weapon Ascension Material** (drop rate: 100% per run at max score). Track your score live via the event UI’s subtle pulse animation—faster pulses = higher precision.
