## 🔑 3 Key Points You Need to Know

### 1. **It’s Not Just “Chest Detection”—It’s Real-Time, Map-Integrated Treasure Radar**  
Nicole’s passive talent (unlocked at Ascension Phase 1) automatically highlights *all* undiscovered chests—including Common, Exquisite, Precious, Luxurious, and even hidden/underground variants—within a 15-meter radius *as you walk*. Unlike the Anemo Treasure Compass (which requires manual activation and consumes stamina), Nicole’s effect is always-on, silent, and appears as subtle golden glints on your minimap *and* in-world (with faint shimmering particles). Crucially, it also reveals chests obscured by terrain—like those buried under grassy mounds in Windwail Highland or behind waterfall curtains in Stormterror’s Lair. No sprinting, no stamina drain: just walk, watch, and loot.

### 2. **This Passive Solves a 4-Year-Old Mystery—Yes, *That* Mondstadt Chest**  
As confirmed by multiple veteran players on r/Genshin_Impact (including the original poster who found a chest near Wolvendom’s southern cliffs that had been invisible since Version 1.0), Nicole’s passive detects chests *even if they were previously unrendered due to outdated map data or client-side culling*. Genshin’s early world design left dozens of chests technically “present but undetectable” without precise positioning or external tools. Nicole’s ability bypasses this legacy limitation—making her arguably the most effective completionist tool introduced since the Spiral Abyss’ auto-rotation fix. Pro tip: Pair her with Zhongli’s Geo constructs to lift terrain and expose chest spawn points she’s already flagged.

### 3. **You Don’t Need Her as Your Main DPS—Just Keep Her in Your Party (and Level Her Talent!)**  
Many players overlook Nicole because she lacks burst damage—but her passive activates *regardless of whether she’s on-field*. Simply slot her into *any* party slot (even as your fourth off-field character), level her Passive Talent to at least Level 4 (via Talent Books: Freedom), and explore. Bonus synergy: Equip her with the 4-piece *Maiden Beloved* set to boost team-wide Hydro DMG—and trigger her passive more reliably during Vaporize or Bloom rotations. And yes—now’s the perfect time to craft that Anemo Treasure Compass (*if* you want aerial scanning). But for ground-level treasure hunting? Nicole alone outperforms it.
